FROM THE PRINCIPAL DESK

Today, the role of a school is not only to pursue academic excellence but also to motivate and empower its students to be lifelong learners, critical thinkers and productive members of an ever-changing global society. More than a decade back Saffron pledged to transform education. The school that is being run under the aegis of well-established Management “SIMON EDUCATIONAL TRUST”. CHAIRMAN’S DESK

The present times call for the students to be multifaceted to increase the prospectus of success and growth in life. At SAINT PAUL CONVENT SCHOOL, we strive to strike balance between academics and co-curricular activities to provide our student an opportunity to discover their scholastic potential and other talents to bring out the best in them.

The education at our institutes is aimed at endowing the students with dexterity to face the challenges of the real world and equipping them with relevant skills to find solutions to overcome those hurdles and come out as winner. Further, much stress is laid upon cultivating the individuality of the learners. We also accept our students to develop deep sense of humanity, collective growth and they work so that they are able to become global citizens in the true sense..

SIMON MATTHEW ASLEY

FROM THE HOSTEL SUPERINTENDENT

The cost of discipline is always less than the price of regret; So self-discipline is the biggest investment for success in life.
My warm greetings to all the SAINT PAUL CONVENT SCHOOL’S hostel students. It gives us mammoth inclination to welcome you all to be a part of SAINT PAUL COVENT SCHOOL’S family that strives to provide students a homely and healthy atmosphere with complete safety and security in the hostel.
At SAINT PAUL CONVENT SCHOOL’S we not only take efforts to provide its students a neat and clean environment and a comfortable place to live in, but encourage them to live in discipline. We understand discipline is the key to success and career building; hence we promote self-discipline among students.
