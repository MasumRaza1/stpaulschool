SAINT PAUL CONVENT SCHOOL

Facilities

LABORATORY

The practical classes have been provided in the daily routine for the students. Kids taught with audio-visual ads.

MEDICAL FACILITIES

In case of illness medical treatment is manage by the school management and the expenses incurred in this connection are realized from the parents.

SCHOOL BUS

The school bus facility is available for different routes. Bus fee charged separately.

CO-CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

To draw forth the hidden and creative talent of the children we conduct co-curricular activities which comprise debate, quiz, art & crafts, calligraphy, cultural activities weekly at the end of the successful session participants are awarded with certificates.

EXCURSION

Students are set on educational trip every year in two different groups preferably to historical places, hill stations, biological parks etc.

MODE OF PAYMENT

a. The fee must be paid in first month on or before 10 of the current month and next month together.
b. After 15th fine will be charged.
c. The fee can be also paid quarterly & half yearly. .

TUTORIAL CLASSES

The institution organizes tutorial classes time-to-time for the needy students to sharpen proficiency with charging extra fee.

PROGRESS REPORT

a. Parents/guardians will be informed of the academic progress of their wards.
b. Examinations are conducted during the academic year:-
1st Periodic- June
Half Yearly- September
2nd Periodic- December
Final Exam- March
c. Weekly test will conducted for all subjects.
d. Promotion is granted on the basis of the whole year’s performance .
