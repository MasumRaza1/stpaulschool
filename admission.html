Admission Rules And regulation

ADMISSION

  • The minimum age for admission in Nursery is three years. Age for admission to other classes is correspondingly. The school has at present classes from Nursery to X. The medium of teaching language is English. The School follows the syllabus and Session of Central Board of Secondary Education (C.B.S.E.) New Delhi.

RESISTRATION

  • Registration form and Prospectus can be available in the School Office from 15th January on Payment of Rs.200/-(Two Hundred Rupees Only) in cash. The cost of prospectus and registration fee shall not be refunded under any circumstances. At the time of Admission the birth certificate & transfer certificate (T.C.) with two (three for hostel) passport size photographs of the candidate is to be submitted in the school.
    • FOR HOSTEL-

    Two passport size photographs of father and mother along with the admission form. Two photographs of that person who will come to meet his/her ward. Registration for admission is opened with effects from 15th January.

    Withdrawals

    • 1.Withdrawals from any class must be preceded by a month’s notice in writing or a month’s fee in lieu of notice.
    • 2.b. All dues will have to be cleared before a transfer certificate is issued.
    • 3.c. Transfer certificate will be issued after two days of receiving the application.
    • 4.d. A fee of Rs.100/- will be charged for issuing a duplicate copy of the transfer certificates and the parent will have to submit an affidavit about the loss of the original certificate.
    • 5.A student can be asked to leave the school on the following grounds:-
      • a) Indiscipline, use of slang language.
      • b) Unsatisfactory progress in work.
      • c) Repeated detention in a class.

    School Uniform

    Boys:
    Sky Blue Printed Shirt, Sky Blue Pant, Blue Socks, Black Shoes, School Belt, Tie and Bag.
    Girls:
    Sky Blue Printed shirt, Sky Blue Skirt, Blue Socks, Black Shoes, School Belt, Tie, White Ribbon and Bag.
    Winter:
    Navy Blue Sweater for all.
