Welcome To Saint Paul Convent

The school exhibits an eye-catching infrastructure, to enhance leering ability and to shape various skills. The school has well developed science laboratories for Biology, Physics, Chemistry, Composite Science, Home Science and Computer Science. Apart from these, Interactive Classrooms are the main features of the school. The school also has a well-stocked Library with books catering to wide range of subjects. It is an English Medium Senior Secondary School Based on CBSE Pattern, New Delhi with strength of 900+ students. It sprawls on an area of 1.5 acres opposite Central Bank Of India, Rehla amidst scenic beauty of the nature ensuring an eco-friendly environment. It cherishes the reputation of a contemporary school deeply rooted in Indian culture and simultaneously responding to the ever changing demands of the global challenges. Comprising of 25 well trained, experienced & dedicated teaching staff and so many supporting staff members, including clerical Staff, security Personnel and assistants.
